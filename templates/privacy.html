Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

We collect minimal data required to provide our business intelligence services. This includes your username, encrypted password, and the business financial data you explicitly enter into the dashboard.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used solely to generate financial visualizations, health scores, and risk alerts. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Isolation

Every user's data is isolated at the database level. Your financial records are private to your account and cannot be accessed by other users.

4. Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including password hashing and secure session management. However, as with any online service, no method is 100% secure.
